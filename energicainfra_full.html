Advisory Services

Project Management
Consultancy (PMC)

Energica Infra provides comprehensive PMC services for solar, wind, and hybrid renewable energy projects across India — from feasibility studies and DPR preparation through EPC management, commissioning, and quality assurance.

Our PMC practice covers the complete project lifecycle for renewable energy developments. We bring together technical expertise, regulatory knowledge, and project management discipline to ensure your project is delivered on time, within budget, and to specification.

With deep experience across RUVNL, GETCO, MPPTCL, APTRANSCO, and KPTCL jurisdictions, Energica Infra navigates state-specific regulatory processes — LTOA, CTU/STU clearances, environmental approvals, and grid connectivity — on behalf of developers and investors.

Our PMC mandate typically commences at the pre-bid stage and continues through commercial operations date (COD), with optional engagement for O&M oversight and performance monitoring in subsequent years.

📐
Feasibility & DPR
Site assessment, solar/wind resource analysis, techno-economic modelling, and Detailed Project Report preparation to bankable standards.
📋
Bid & Contract Management
Technical specifications, RfP/RfQ preparation, tender evaluation, vendor qualification, and EPC contract negotiation and award.
🔬
Quality Assurance & Testing
Independent quality inspections, factory acceptance testing, site acceptance testing, commissioning oversight, and performance ratio validation.
📊
Progress Certification
Milestone-linked progress certification, invoice verification, and compliance reporting to lenders, investors, and government authorities.

Financial Advisory

Project Finance &
Capital Structuring

Energica Infra structures and facilitates debt and equity financing for renewable energy projects — from term loans and green bonds to co-investments, bridging finance, and syndicated facilities across India's capital markets.

Energica Infra's project finance practice provides end-to-end financial advisory for renewable energy developers. We identify, structure, and facilitate the optimal mix of debt and equity to bring projects to financial close efficiently.

Our relationships span DFIs (PFC, REC, IREDA), public sector banks, private lenders, NBFCs, and international development finance institutions. We also facilitate equity co-investments from institutional investors, family offices, and strategic partners seeking exposure to India's energy transition.

We assist clients in preparing bankable financial models, information memoranda, and lender presentations — and represent them through due diligence, term sheet negotiation, and financial close.

Energy Storage Advisory

BESS & Energy
Storage Sizing

Energica Infra provides Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) advisory — from techno-economic analysis and optimal sizing through technology selection, grid code compliance, and integration with solar and wind assets across India's hybrid energy landscape.

India's push for 24x7 renewable power supply and the growth of Round-the-Clock (RTC) and hybrid tenders have made Battery Energy Storage Systems central to project design. Energica Infra's BESS advisory helps developers and investors optimise storage configurations for maximum commercial value.

Our team conducts detailed load flow analysis, generation dispatch modelling, and round-trip efficiency assessment to recommend the optimal battery chemistry, capacity (MWh), and power rating (MW) for each project configuration. We evaluate LFP, NMC, and flow battery technologies against project-specific cost, cycle life, and safety requirements.

We support clients through CERC/SERC grid code compliance, ISTS waiver applications for hybrid BESS projects, and integration with SCADA and energy management systems for real-time dispatch optimisation.

🔋
Optimal BESS Sizing
Techno-economic modelling to determine the optimal MWh capacity, C-rate, and duration for your specific project configuration and offtake requirements.
⚡
Hybrid System Design
Integrated solar + wind + BESS design with dispatch optimisation to maximise CUF, meet scheduling obligations, and comply with ISTS/PGCIL requirements.
🔬
Technology Evaluation
Independent evaluation of LFP, NMC, sodium-ion, and flow battery technologies — covering cycle life, round-trip efficiency, CAPEX/OPEX, and safety.
📡
Grid Code & Compliance
CERC/SERC grid code compliance advisory, ISTS waiver applications for hybrid projects, and energy management system integration support.

Operations Advisory

SCADA & O&M
Advisory Services

Energica Infra provides SCADA integration, remote monitoring, and O&M advisory for solar, wind, and hybrid renewable projects — combining real-time performance analytics with preventive maintenance protocols to maximise generation and asset life.

Operational excellence is what separates a performing asset from an underperforming one. Energica Infra's SCADA and O&M advisory practice helps plant operators and asset owners implement robust monitoring frameworks and maintenance protocols that protect generation yield and financial returns.

We design and implement SCADA architectures that consolidate data from inverters, weather stations, MFMs, and grid meters into a unified operational view — enabling real-time performance tracking, fault detection, and automated alerts. Our dashboards are designed for both plant operators and remote asset managers.

On the O&M side, we develop customised maintenance schedules, performance benchmarks, and KPI frameworks. We also conduct independent generation audits and performance ratio assessments for lenders, investors, and asset owners seeking objective performance verification.

📡
SCADA Architecture
Design and implementation of SCADA systems integrating inverters, trackers, MFMs, and grid meters — with real-time dashboards and automated alarm management.
📊
Performance Analytics
Generation performance benchmarking, specific yield analysis, performance ratio tracking, and loss cascade analysis to identify and rectify yield gaps.
🔧
Preventive Maintenance
O&M framework design covering preventive maintenance schedules, spares management, contractor oversight, and HSE compliance for plant operators.
🔎
Independent Audits
Third-party generation audits and performance ratio assessments for lenders, institutional investors, and asset owners seeking independent verification.
